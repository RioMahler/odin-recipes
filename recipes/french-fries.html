French Fries

Description

Air fryed French Fries. Easy, uncomplicated, tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of frozen French fries
  • Optional: Sunflower oil

Steps

  1. Preheat Air Fryer: Set to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place fries in the air fryer basket in a single layer (don’t overcrowd). Add a spoon of sunflower oil for extra crispness.
  3. Air fry for 15–18 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through.
  4. Serve.
