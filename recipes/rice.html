Rice

Description

Plain rice. That's all.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups water

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed rice and water
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan with a tight-fitting lid, and let it simmer gently for about 15-20 minutes, or until the water is absorbed and the rice is tender.
  5. Turn off the heat and let the rice sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Then, remove the lid and fluff the rice gently with a fork.
